LogoBadge Setup Guide

Follow these steps to add your Google Reviews badge directly under your store logo.

LogoBadge is installed on your store. Now let's set it up.
1

Open Theme Editor

From your Shopify admin, go to:

Online Store → Themes → Customize
2

Enable the App Embed

In the Theme Editor, click the puzzle piece icon (App embeds) in the top-left toolbar.

Find "Google Review Badge" and toggle it ON.

The badge will automatically appear directly under your store logo. No dragging needed.
3

Paste Your Google Maps Link

In the badge settings panel, paste your Google Maps URL:

  1. Open Google Maps in your browser
  2. Search for your business name
  3. Click Share
  4. Click Copy link
  5. Paste it in the "Google Maps URL" field

Your real Google rating and review count will appear automatically.

4

Adjust Settings (Optional)

Customize the badge appearance:

  • Badge Size — Small, Medium, or Large
  • Gap from Logo — How close the badge sits to your logo
5

Save

Click Save in the top-right corner of the Theme Editor. Your Google Reviews badge is now live on your store.

The badge appears on every page of your store, always centered under your logo.
