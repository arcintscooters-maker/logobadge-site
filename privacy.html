Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

LogoBadge ("we", "our", or "us") is a Shopify app developed by ShipAnywhere. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

LogoBadge is a lightweight theme extension. We collect minimal information:

  • Store information: Your Shopify store URL and name, provided during app installation through Shopify's standard OAuth process.
  • App settings: Configuration data you enter in the Theme Editor (star rating, review count, Google Maps URL, display preferences).

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

LogoBadge does not collect, store, or process:

  • Customer personal data
  • Order information
  • Payment or financial data
  • Browsing behavior or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Product or inventory data

3. How We Use Information

The store information collected during installation is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your app installation
  • Provide the Google Review Badge functionality on your storefront
  • Communicate important app updates or changes

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Your configuration data stays within your Shopify theme and is not transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Storage and Security

LogoBadge operates entirely as a Shopify Theme App Extension. All configuration data is stored within your Shopify theme settings, managed by Shopify's infrastructure. We do not maintain separate databases or servers storing your data.

6. Data Retention

When you uninstall LogoBadge, all app-related settings are removed from your theme. We do not retain any data after uninstallation.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your app configuration data through the Shopify Theme Editor
  • Modify or delete your settings at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data
  • Request information about what data we hold (contact us below)

8. GDPR Compliance

Since LogoBadge does not collect or process personal data of your customers, GDPR data processing obligations are minimal. We comply with all applicable data protection regulations.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: admin@inlinex.com.sg
  • Website: inlinex.com.sg
